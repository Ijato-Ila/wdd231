The High

A site plan for a digital hub celebrating Nigerian fashion designers, stylists, brands, and trends.

Site Name

Site Name
The High
Why this name?
"The High" a place that lifts Nigerian fashion to a higher stage. It's short, brandable, and works well on social platforms and as a domain (examples: thehigh.ng, thehigh.com).

Site Purpose

The High will be an editorial and portfolio platform that showcases Nigerian fashion talent and industry activity. Primary goals:

  • Feature designers, stylists, creative directors and brands through profile pages and lookbooks.
  • Publish event listings for fashion shows, pop-ups, and workshops.
  • Provide style guides, trend reports, and buying guides.
  • Host an online gallery of outfits & accessories with lightbox preview and tagging.
  • Offer a contact/collaboration form for media, buyers, and creative partnerships.

User Scenarios (Who visits & why)

  1. Emerging designer: "How can I submit my lookbook or get featured on The High?" needs clear submission and contact flow.
  2. Fashion buyer: "Which designers are showing at the Lagos Fashion Week pop-up next month?" needs event listings and designer profiles.
  3. Fashion enthusiast: "What are the current street style trends in Nigeria this season?" needs trend articles and galleries.

Typography

Selected web fonts and where they'll be applied.

Montserrat (Headings, UI)
Used for H1/H2/H3 and UI elements like buttons and navigation. Clean, modern, strong presence.
Lora (Body)
Readable serif for article body text and captions, pairs well with Montserrat for an editorial feel.

Fallbacks: headings: system-ui, sans-serif; body: Georgia, serif. The inline CSS demonstrates these choices in this site plan document.
